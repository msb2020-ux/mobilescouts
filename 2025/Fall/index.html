Avengers Quest 2025

The 2025 Mobile Area Camporee — Avengers Quest — brought Scouts from across the council together at Camp Maubila for a weekend of adventure, teamwork, and scouting excellence.

170 Scouts from 16 units competed across 9 events over 3 days of patrol-based challenges, ceremonies, and night activities.
