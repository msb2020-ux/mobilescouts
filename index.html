About the Event

Camp Maubila · October 23–25, 2026
This is not a Halloween party.

This is a skills-based, story-driven camporee built around one idea:

Daylight prepares the camp. Night tests it.

Patrols will train, build, navigate, and compete through structured daytime challenges designed to sharpen real Scout skills — leadership, first aid, pioneering, navigation, marksmanship, and teamwork under pressure.

Competition officially begins Saturday mid-day.

However, Saturday morning offers optional skill intensives in Orienteering and Pioneering. Patrols who attend may recognize significant advantages later — especially after dark.

After sunset, the experience changes.

Signal hunts. Night navigation. Moving in the woods. Leadership immersive competitive scenarios.

The most important challenges unfold in the dark, and activities may continue until approximately 2:00 AM Sunday.

This event is designed for all Scouts, but it will enable older Scouts to test patrol cohesion, and to reward preparation.

Arrive Friday night — or very early Saturday — to be fully prepared.
